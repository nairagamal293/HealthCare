Our Story

We Provide The Best Quality Healthcare

Founded in 1998, MediCare Center has grown from a small community clinic to a leading multi-specialty healthcare provider. Our journey has been guided by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and compassionate patient care.

Today, we serve over 100,000 patients annually with a team of 120+ board-certified physicians across 15+ specialties, supported by state-of-the-art technology and facilities.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted healthcare provider, transforming lives through exceptional clinical outcomes and innovative care models.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of our community.

Our Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Teamwork guide everything we do at MediCare Center.
