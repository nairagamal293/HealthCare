General Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our main clinic is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Some departments may have different hours, so we recommend calling ahead for specific services.

For after-hours emergencies, our on-call physician is available by phone at (234) 567-892.

Do I need an appointment to be seen?

While appointments are preferred to minimize wait times, we do accept walk-ins for urgent care needs. For routine check-ups and non-urgent visits, we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment in advance.

Same-day appointments are often available for urgent needs. You can check availability through our patient portal or by calling our appointment line.

Insurance & Payments

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including:

  • Medicare
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Humana
  • Kaiser Permanente
  • Most Medicaid plans

For a complete list of accepted insurance providers, please visit our Insurance Information page or contact our billing department at (234) 567-893.

What payment methods do you accept?

We offer multiple convenient payment options:

  • Cash - Accepted at all locations
  • Credit/Debit Cards - Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Personal Checks - With valid photo ID
  • HSA/FSA Cards - Health Savings and Flexible Spending Accounts
  • Mobile Payments - Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Payment Plans - Available for qualifying patients

Payment is expected at the time of service unless prior arrangements have been made with our billing department.

Appointments

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through several convenient methods:

Online

Use our Appointment Request form available 24/7

By Phone

Call our appointment line at (234) 567-891 during business hours

Patient Portal

Existing patients can schedule through our secure portal

In Person

Visit our reception desk during business hours

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that schedules change, but we kindly request at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the time slot to other patients in need of care.

Cancellation fees may apply:

  • Regular appointments: $25 for late cancellations (less than 24 hours)
  • Procedures/specialty visits: $50 for late cancellations
  • No-show appointments: Full visit fee may be charged

To cancel or reschedule, please call our office during business hours. Multiple late cancellations may result in restrictions on future scheduling.

Services

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer secure video consultations for many types of visits through our MediCare Virtual Care platform. Telemedicine appointments are ideal for:

  • Follow-up visits
  • Medication management
  • Chronic condition monitoring
  • Mental health consultations
  • Minor acute conditions (colds, rashes, etc.)

To schedule a virtual visit, select "Telemedicine" when booking your appointment. You'll receive instructions for connecting via email prior to your visit.

Note: Some services require in-person evaluation. Your provider will determine if telemedicine is appropriate for your needs.

What emergency services do you provide?

For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.

Our facility offers urgent care services for non-life-threatening conditions during business hours, including:

  • Minor injuries (sprains, minor cuts)
  • Fevers and infections
  • Allergic reactions (non-severe)
  • Asthma attacks (mild to moderate)
  • Dehydration
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Ear or eye infections
  • Minor burns

Our on-call physician is available after hours for urgent medical concerns at (234) 567-894. For true emergencies, we recommend visiting a hospital emergency department.
